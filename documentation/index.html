Getting started

Start your documentation template by editing one of the starter files. It won't take more than 15 minutes to start. Read the step-by-step guide:


  • Prepare your directory

    Upload the files in the karakata.zip to your public_html/www folder on your webapp.

  • Setup your database

    Create a database on your server.

    How do i create my database?

    Here is a video on how to create database in cpanel https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=YbIn--iNmKE

    Open the .env file and update your DATABASE credentials

  • Begin installation

    Navigate to http://YOUR_URL.COM/install, to begin registration. You would be taken to the install screen

    Enter your website settings and details including your purchase code, and submit

    I do not see this screen!

    If you do not see this screen. A list of requirements and instructions that needs to be fulfilled will be shown on the screen before proceeding.
  • That's all

    Really that's all. you should see a success message informing you of installation success.

    You can login to your app to begin customization

App updates!

To update the app unzip the folder called updates and upload to your server using the same folder structure. When asked if to overwrite, choose yes.
After upload is complete. navigate to http://YOUR_URL/update
